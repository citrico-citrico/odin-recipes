Cheeseburger

Description

Traditional american double cheeseburger. An easy to make americana staple. The template from which you may experiment with other recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 hamburger bun, split
  • 1/3 pound ground beef
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 4 processed cheese singles (such as kraft)
  • 1 tablespoon thousand island salad dressing
  • 1 slice tomato
  • 1 leaf lettuce
  • 1 slcie onion
